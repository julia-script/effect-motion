---
title: Particle fountain
description: A confetti fountain from a single ParticleField emitter
---

A fountain from the [particle system](/docs/concepts/particles): one
`Particles.emitter` streams particles up in a narrow cone, gravity arcs them
back down, and each shrinks and fades as it ages. You author the *distribution* —
speed, angle, life, and size as ranges — and the seed draws each particle.

The whole spray is **one instance and one animator fiber**, not one per
particle: the particles are packed state advanced as a batch, so the frame
barrier does the same work for a thousand as for one. And because the only
randomness is the scene's seeded `Random`, the fountain is fully
deterministic — scrub the timeline back and forth and every jet retraces its
exact path.
