A ball. A ledge. A star it cannot reach.

It jumps — short. It backs up, runs harder, jumps higher — and meets the
wall at exactly ledge height, then does the slow cartoon slide down it.
It sits there, visibly deflated, and thinks. Then it leaves. And comes
back **pushing a box**.

Everything here is ordinary scene code: squash-and-stretch is two
concurrent tweens keeping the sole planted, the jumps are an arc helper
(rise `easeOutQuad`, fall `easeInQuad`), the wall-slide is one sad
`easeInSine`, the celebration is a `fork` so the star's sparkle
`stagger` flies while the ball keeps bouncing, and the moral arrives as
rich text.
