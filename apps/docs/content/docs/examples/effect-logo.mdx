---
title: The Effect Logo
description: The layered mark rebuilt as a real 3D scene — flat plates, a pitched camera, and a HUD wordmark
---

Instead of tracing the logo's SVG paths, this scene rebuilds what the mark
actually depicts: three square plates stacked in space. Each plate is a
`Rect` laid flat (`rotX: π/2`) and turned 45° in the ground plane
(`rotY: π/4`); a camera panned up and pitched down supplies the isometric
foreshortening, so the diamonds come from the projection rather than drawn
geometry.

The plates spring into the stack bottom-up with `Scene.stagger`, and the
wordmark lives on a `Hud` layer — screen-space text that ignores the camera
entirely.
