---
title: Moon, Moth, Window
description: A tiny animated story about ambition, glass, and meeting halfway
---

A moth attempts an unreasonable commute, discovers a window, and has one
dignified `bonk.` The moon notices and solves the distance problem itself.

This is an authored scene rather than a feature sampler. Groups turn the moon
and moth into actors, a background wing loop gives the flight texture, and the
story advances through deliberately timed motion, pauses, and changing captions.
